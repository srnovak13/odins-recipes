Classic French Lentil Salad

A fresh and wholesome salad!

Made with earthy Puy lentils, carrots, shallots, and an herb-dijon vinaigrette

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Puy Lentils (or green or brown)
  • 3 cups of Water (or vegetable or chicken broth)
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • 1 large Carrot
  • 1 small Shallot
  • 4 tbsp of finely chopped Parsley
  • 1 tsp of fresh Thyme
  • 1-2 cloves of Garlic
  • 1/2 tbsp of Dijon Mustard
  • 1 tsp of Honey
  • 2 tbsp of fresh Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 cup of Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Place the lentils in a fine mesh strainer and rinse them thoroughly. Place the lentils in a medium pot
  2. Add the water or broth, bay leaf, and bring to a boil. Once boiling, turn the heat down and simmer for about 20 minutes
  3. Add the diced carrots and cook for 5 more minutes, until lentils and carrots are tender
  4. Strain, remove bay leaf and let cool to room temperature
  5. Once lentils are cooled, add chopped shallot, parsley, thyme, garlic, dijon mustard, honey, lemon juice and olive oil
  6. Season with salt to pepper to taste and toss to combine
  7. Serve with a soft cheese such as Brie, crumbled goat cheese, or blue cheese
